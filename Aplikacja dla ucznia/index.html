CHECK YOUR MATH SKILL

How it works? To start click "RESULT BOX". When 2 random numbers appear, you have to make calculations for yourself, and then write down your result into result box. To chcek if your answer is correct, click "check your result" button. When background on result input turns green, it indicate that your math was correct, but if it's red, that means that you made a mistake and your calculations were wrong. To do next caluluation click "RESULT BOX". Have fun :)
